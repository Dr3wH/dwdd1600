About

Drew Hansen is Utah artist currently studying graphic design at UVU. His work includes Graphic design, Painting, and Sculpture. Hansen's work often uses materials like wool and yarn to convey personal messages. He is currently working on multiple projects that will hopefullly be shown at art shows in the future.
